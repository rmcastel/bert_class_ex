  (c)  Contents of compliance program.The compliance program shall, at a minimum:  (1)  Provide for a system of internal controls to assure ongoing compliance;  (2)  Provide for independent testing for compliance to be conducted by bank personnel or by an outside party;  (3)  Designate an individual or individuals responsible for coordinating and monitoring day-to-day compliance; and  (4)  Provide training for appropriate personnel.
