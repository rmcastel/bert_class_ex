Fee appraisers If an appraisal is prepared by a fee appraiser the appraiser shall be engaged directly by the regulated institution or its agent and have no direct or indirect interest financial or otherwise in the property or the transaction A regulated institution also may accept an appraisal that was prepared by an appraiser engaged directly by another financial services institution if The appraiser has no direct or indirect interest financial or otherwise in the property or the transaction and The regulated institution determines that the appraisal conforms to the requirements of this subpart and is otherwise acceptable
