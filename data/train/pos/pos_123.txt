Paragraph of this section shall not apply to contracts for the sale for cash of securities that are priced after 4 30 p m Eastern time on the date the securities are priced and that are sold by an issuer to an underwriter pursuant to a firm commitment underwritten offering registered under the Securities Act of 1933 15 U S C 77a et seq or sold to an initial purchaser by an FDIC supervised institution participating in the offering An FDIC supervised institution shall not effect or enter into a contract for the purchase or sale of the securities that provides for payment of funds and delivery of securities later than the fourth business day after the date of the contract unless otherwise expressly agreed to by the parties at the time of the transaction
