General rule The opt out requirements in 332 7 and 332 10 do not apply when you provide nonpublic personal information to a nonaffiliated third party to perform services for you or functions on your behalf if you Provide the initial notice in accordance with 332 4 and Enter into a contractual agreement with the third party that prohibits the third party from disclosing or using the information other than to carry out the purposes for which you disclosed the information including use under an exception in 332 14 or 332 15 in the ordinary course of business to carry out those purposes Example If you disclose nonpublic personal information under this section to a financial institution with which you perform joint marketing your contractual agreement with that institution meets the requirements of paragraph of this section if it prohibits the institution from disclosing or using the nonpublic personal information except as necessary to carry out the joint marketing or under an exception in 332 14 or 332 15 in the ordinary course of business to carry out that joint marketing
