Transfer of servicing rights The FDIC supervised institution shall notify the Administrator of FEMA or the Administrator of FEMA s designee of any change in the servicer of a loan described in paragraph of this section within 60 days after the effective date of the change This notice may be provided electronically if electronic transmission is satisfactory to the Administrator or his or her designee Upon any change in the servicing of a loan described in paragraph of this section the duty to provide notice under this paragraph shall transfer to the transferee servicer
