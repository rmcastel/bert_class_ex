g Duration of consumer s opt out direction 1 A consumer s direction to opt out under this section is effective until the consumer revokes it in writing or if the consumer agrees electronically 2 When a customer relationship terminates the customer s opt out direction continues to apply to the nonpublic personal information that you collected during or related to that relationship If the individual subsequently establishes a new customer relationship with you the opt out direction that applied to the former relationship does not apply to the new relationship
