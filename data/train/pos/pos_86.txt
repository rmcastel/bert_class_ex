General Notwithstanding any Federal or State law other than the Flood Disaster Protection Act of 1973 as amended 42 U S C 4001 4129 any FDIC supervised institution or a servicer acting on its behalf may charge a reasonable fee for determining whether the building or mobile home securing the loan is located or will be located in a special flood hazard area A determination fee may also include but is not limited to a fee for life of loan monitoring
