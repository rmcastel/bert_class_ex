Appraisal management services means one or more of the following Recruiting selecting and retaining appraisers Contracting with State certified or State licensed appraisers to perform appraisal assignments Managing the process of having an appraisal performed including providing administrative services such as receiving appraisal orders and appraisal reports submitting completed appraisal reports to creditors and secondary market participants collecting fees from creditors and secondary market participants for services provided and paying appraisers for services performed and Reviewing and verifying the work of appraisers
