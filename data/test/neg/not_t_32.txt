Debt security means any security such as a bond debenture note or any other similar instrument that evidences a liability of the issuer including any security of this type that is convertible into stock or a similar security and fractional or participation interests in one or more of any of the foregoing provided however that securities issued by an investment company registered under the Investment Company Act of 1940 15 U S C 80a 1 et seq shall not be included in this definition
