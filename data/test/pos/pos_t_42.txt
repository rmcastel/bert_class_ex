Notification to Federal Reserve At least fourteen days prior to the first reserve computation period that an institution intends to establish an IBF it shall notify the Federal Reserve bank of the district in which it is located of its intent Such notification shall include a statement of intention by the institution that it will comply with the rules of this part concerning IBFs including restrictions on sources and uses of funds and recordkeeping and accounting requirements Failure to comply with the requirements of this part shall subject the institution to reserve requirements under this part or result in the revocation of the institution s ability to operate an IBF
