Depository institution means Any insured bank as defined in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act 12 U S C 1813 or any bank that is eligible to apply to become an insured bank under section 5 of such Act 12 U S C 1815 Any savings bank or mutual savings bank as defined in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act 12 U S C 1813 Any insured credit union as defined in section 101 of the Federal Credit Union Act 12 U S C 1752 or any credit union that is eligible to apply to become an insured credit union under section 201 of such Act 12 U S C 1781 Any member as defined in section 2 of the Federal Home Loan Bank Act 12 U S C 1422 and Any insured institution as defined in section 401 of the National Housing Act 12 U S C 1724 or any institution which is eligible to apply to become an insured institution under section 403 of such Act 12 U S C 1726 Depository institution does not include international organizations such as the World Bank the Inter American Development Bank and the Asian Development Bank
