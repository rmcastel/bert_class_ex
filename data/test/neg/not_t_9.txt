Government security means A security that is a direct obligation of or obligation guaranteed as to principal and interest by the United States A security that is issued or guaranteed by a corporation in which the United States has a direct or indirect interest and which is designated by the Secretary of the Treasury for exemption as necessary or appropriate in the public interest or for the protection of investors A security issued or guaranteed as to principal and interest by any corporation whose securities are designated by statute specifically naming the corporation to constitute exempt securities within the meaning of the laws administered by the Securities and Exchange Commission or Any put call straddle option or privilege on a security described in paragraph or of this section other than a put call straddle option or privilege that is traded on one or more national securities exchanges or for which quotations are disseminated through an automated quotation system operated by a registered securities association
